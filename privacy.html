Data Collection

The CENTIence Network operates on a zero-knowledge proof system, ensuring minimal data collection. We only store:

  • Public wallet addresses
  • Smart contract interactions
  • Encrypted message hashes

Token Privacy

SPENCE token transactions are processed through privacy-preserving protocols. Your token balance and transactions are visible only to authorized wallet addresses.

Encryption Standards

We implement military-grade encryption for all network communications:

  • AES-256 for data at rest
  • End-to-end encryption for messages
  • Quantum-resistant algorithms for sensitive operations

Your Rights

As a CENTIence Network user, you have the right to:

  • Request data export
  • Delete your account
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Modify privacy settings
